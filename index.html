Crystal Collector

Rules to the Game:

You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount to your "total crystals collected" score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. At the start of a new game each crystal will auto generate a new value.
